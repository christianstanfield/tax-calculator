Fork this codepen and finish the "Tax Calculator" that compares the outcomes for a given income of two different fictional tax plans.

The "Foo" Plan

  • Any income below $100,000 is taxed at 10%
  • Any income from $100,001 to $200,000 is taxed at 15%
  • Any income above $200,001 is taxed at 20%

The "Bar" Plan

  • Any income below $100,000 is taxed at 13%
  • Any income from $100,001 to $200,000 is taxed at 15%
  • Any income above $200,001 is taxed at 16%

Requirements

  1. Align the three inputs so they are in a row, not stacked.
  2. Create a submit handler that does the calculation and updates the fields.
  3. Visually highlight the lower of the two tax burdens.
  4. Deliver a message to the user about which tax plan offers the lower burden.
  5. If time permits, write a few lines of CSS to improve the look-and-feel, either in your own style or on-brand for Barackobama.com.

Only work with whole dollars. jQuery is already included, and you may use anything else you like or change anything about the given markup. Explain any other assumptions in comments and do your best.
